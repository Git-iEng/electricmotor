Explore Types

DC Motors

Motors that convert direct current electrical energy into mechanical energy. Often preferred for applications requiring fine speed control and high starting torque, such as in traction, cranes, and rolling mills.

Synchronous Motors

AC motors where the rotor rotates at exactly the same speed as the rotating magnetic field (synchronous speed). Used in applications requiring precise, constant speed , like high-speed compressors, pumps, and fans.

3 Phase Induction Motors

The most common type of AC motor, valued for its simplicity, ruggedness, and low maintenance . Used extensively in general-purpose industrial drives, including pumps, conveyors, and machine tools.

Single Phase Induction Motors

Motors that operate on a single-phase AC power supply, typically used for smaller industrial and domestic applications . Found in packaging machines, small compressors, ventilation systems, and domestic appliances.

Ball & Roller Bearings

We supply a variety of precision bearings to minimize friction in rotating parts. Our focus on very high quality ensures reduced wear and tear, critical for the long-term reliability of industrial machines and motors.
